About Mi Games Now

Original lightweight browser games with clear rules, strategy notes, design diaries, and fast play sessions.

Mi Games Now publishes compact original browser games and the strategy notes that make them easier to understand. The site is built around playable prototypes, scoring explanations, update logs, and short design essays.

The project avoids copied game catalogs and focuses on original mechanics that can run quickly in a modern browser without heavy downloads.

Editorial standards

Pages are planned around usefulness first: clear titles, readable structure, original examples, and updates when information changes. See the editorial policy for the full review approach.
